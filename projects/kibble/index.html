Kibble

Set in the dilapidated back lot of an apartment complex, a foggy wasteland of garbage and torn-up concrete, Kibble tells the story of a morcel of dog food that springs to life, only to find himself in a gnarled dog bowl sitting at the base of the colossal maw of a doghouse, merely moments from sure demise . . .
